Master Classes

21st Century Design with Don Norman

From this master class, I learned about the evolving role of design in addressing global challenges, including its potential for both positive and negative impact.

AI-Powered UX Design

The master class covered the current applications of AI in UX Design, the challenges of integrating AI, why AI won't replace UX Designers, the essential skills for staying relevant, and the importance of maintaining a future-open mindset as the design landscape evolves.

How (and When) To Run A Design Sprint

In this master class, I learned about the background and history of Design Sprint, its step-by-step process, real examples, and when and how to run a Design Sprint. These skills can greatly enhance project value and facilitate faster decision-making within teams, aiding in feature development, marketing plans, and actionable insights.

Games UX Foundations

Through this Master Class, I gained insights into Games UX Design, focusing on managing friction, understanding player motivations, exploring habit loops, collaboration between game and UX design, and the importance of player archetypes. This course is perfect for those interested in Games UX and designers transitioning from general UX to Games UX design.
