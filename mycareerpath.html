Persona

  • Persona plays an important role in the UX desiging process
  • A user persona is a fictional representation of the ideal User of the Product.
  • As a UX designer, the design process starts with conducting user research, building empathy with the target users and identifying exactly what they need from the product we are designing.

Linda Cooper and John Hans

Interviewed Multi-Taskers for a Productivity App called One Task. I discussed how they do their day to day tasks and how they manage completion within deadlines. After talking to around 10 people, I drafted my Pesonas which summed up all the characteristics of the users I interviewed.

Mary Parker, Peter Smith & Joshua Bing

While re-designing a corporate Library Portal intranet website, I got an opportunity to interview the employees of the company who used the Library. It was interesting to note that, with all the new technology in hand, Users sometimes prefer the experience of reading a Book physically. I interviewed dozens of employees and could map their behaviours to three distinct characters.

Josie Lane

While working on a Website and App which has a variety of Audio and Video Content, I interviewed Users who used different Yoga and meditation Apps and discussed on the pain points. Most of them preferred offline content and listening to the audio rather then staring at a screen.
