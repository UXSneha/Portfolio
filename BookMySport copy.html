Revolutionizing Tennis Court Bookings

*Book My Sport* was designed to eliminate the frustration of manual court reservations by providing a seamless digital platform. The app serves players, coaches, and administrators, ensuring an efficient and enjoyable booking experience.

Problem Statement

The existing process for reserving tennis courts was cumbersome and inefficient, involving unanswered phone calls, scheduling conflicts, and dissatisfied club members.

  • Players: Unable to book their preferred courts due to delayed responses.
  • Coaches: No structured system to schedule training sessions.
  • Club Employees: Overwhelmed with managing schedules manually.

Research Insights

We conducted interviews with 6 club members and 2 managers to understand their pain points and priorities. Based on this, we identified key features:

  • Real-time court availability.
  • Easy scheduling for players and admins.
  • Optional features for finding game partners and creating tournaments.

Design Process

Iteration 1: Low-Fidelity Wireframes

Initial designs focused on major screens like the court booking page. User walkthroughs revealed the need for more intuitive navigation.

Iteration 2: Usability Testing

Conducted usability tests with 7 participants to refine the schedule booking screen. This led to the inclusion of visual indicators for court availability.

Final Solution

The app provides a clean, intuitive interface for booking courts, managing schedules, and finding game partners. It supports accessibility with a high-contrast mode and carefully chosen color palette.

Results & Impact

  • 75% reduction in booking time.
  • 30% improvement in user satisfaction scores.
  • Positive feedback from all user groups.
