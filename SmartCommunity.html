    PAIN POINTS AND USERS

    Player:

    "I have to call the Tennis Club for reserving a Court, most of the times my calls are unattended and when they call me back my favourite Courts are already booked!"

    "Is there a way I can train members in the sport I am good at?"

    Club Employee:

    "We get calls all the time and we are unable to keep track of the scheduling of Courts, also the regular players in the Club keep complaining of not able to get the Courts the prefer"

    In order to address the above pain-points I came up with the following Users

  • Members - Players who belong to a club and want to book slots in a Court
  • Non-Members - Guest Players who want to book the Court one time
  • Admin - Management/Employees of the Club who will book the courts on behalf of Players
  • Coach - Certified trainers who are the members of the club and would like to train Players


    • USER RESEARCH AND ADDITIONAL FEATURES

    After discussion with 6 members of the Club and 2 of the Club Managers, we decided to add few more features to the app like Creating a Tournament, finding a game partner and creating game groups within the App

    Each user had a different set access to various activities that can be performed by the App

    There would be 4 different Personas using this App as per the checklist
