Book My Sport

Tennis Court Booking App

When few of my developer friends faced with a problem of calling and booking a court at a nearby club, we decided on creating an App that would solve this problem. We also came to know that all the members of the Tennis club as well as the Club team had the same issue. So we decided to tackle this problem by creating an App.

Player:

"I have to call the Tennis Club for reserving a Court, most of the times my calls are unattended and when they call me back my favourite Courts are already booked!"

Club Employee:

"We get calls all the time and we are unable to keep track of the scheduling of Courts, also the regular players in the Club keep complaining of not able to get the Courts the prefer"

PROBLEM STATEMENT:
Court booking App for easy scheduling - Both the Players as well as Club Employee should have access to booking the Court


PROJECT DURATION:
3 Weeks - Design and Development

DESIGN TOOLS:
Sketch, Balsamiq

ROLE:
Product Design, Interaction Design, User Reasearch, Usability Testing

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

Now that I know the problem statement, I started researching the existing scheduling products and interviewed various users on the pain points of the apps they are currently using. I created a Low-Fidelity mockup and discussed with the users as to how they would like to go about the User Journey.

  • The flow usually begins as the User logs in as Player or Club Employee
  • At this stage he can view all the eligible game areas they can Book
  • Each club has many courts and the users wanted to see a gist of the events happening in each court
  • Based on the schedules of the court the user would then choose the court
  • The time and date choosing screen was where most of the users wanted innovation
