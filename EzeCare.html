Enhancing Medical Practice Experience with MediConnect

In this project, I worked on exploring the process and solutions aimed at addressing the challenges and pain points associated with the experience of visiting a medical practitioner. The focus is on enhancing user experience for both patients and healthcare providers through the development of an innovative healthcare application.

Project Duration:

1 Week - Research & Design

Tools Used:

Figma, Balsamiq

Role:

Product Design, User Experience Design, User Research

Deliverables

Initial Mockups, High Fidelity

Brain Dump (Initial Brainstorming and Idea Generation Sessions):

During the brain dump stage, I collaborated with a multidisciplinary team. We engaged in brainstorming sessions to generate ideas and concepts for the healthcare application. Together, we gathered thoughts, explored possibilities, and generated innovative solutions to address the challenges and pain points identified in the user experience research.

Collaboration with Multidisciplinary Team:

This collaborative effort allowed us to leverage diverse perspectives and expertise. Working together, we fostered creativity, identified opportunities, and brainstormed solutions that were informed by a deep understanding of user needs and industry best practices. The collaboration with the team was instrumental in generating valuable insights and ideas for the project.
